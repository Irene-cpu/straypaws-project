Spread Awareness, Save Lives

Why Awareness Matters

Many stray cats go unnoticed because their struggles are invisible to most people.
Education and awareness help communities recognize the problem and become part of the solution

Simple Ways To Make A Difference

You don't need special skills to help. Small actions can create real impact.

Post adoption stories or rescue updates to reach more people.

Start conversation with friends, family or classmates.

Report Injured Stray Cats

help connect cats in need with rescue groups.

Support Local Rescuers

Promote trusted shelters and volunteer efforts.
